💜 BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) 💜

💜 BT21 characters

BTS is an internationally famous South Korean boy group formed in 2013 under the agency Big Hit. The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, V, and Jungkook. They are known for the meaningful music they create and the amazing messages behind them.

They have broken multiple records, like most streamed on Spotify, having over 16.3 billion streams, and achieved most views in 24 hours for their song "Butter" with 108 million views. Most recently, their new album "Arirang" became the biggest first-day streaming album on Apple Music and Spotify, with their lead track "Swim" topping in 90 regions (as of 2026).
