💜 BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) 💜

💜 BT21 characters


BTS is an internationally famous South Korean boy group formed in 2013 under Big Hit. Members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, V, and Jungkook. They are known for meaningful music and messages.

Records include most streamed on Spotify (16.3 billion streams), 24-hour record for "Butter" (108 million views), and new album "Arirang" topping Apple Music & Spotify in 90 regions with lead track "Swim" (2026).
