About BTS

BTS is a South Korean boy group that debuted in 2013. They are known for their music, powerful performances, and meaningful messages about self-love and growth.

Over the years, BTS has become one of the most popular music groups in the world, gaining millions of fans called ARMY.They have broken multiple records, like most streamed on Spotify, having over 16.3 billion streams, and achieved most views in 24 hours for their song "Butter" with 108 million views. Most recently, their new album "Arirang" became the biggest first-day streaming album on Apple Music and Spotify, with their lead track "Swim" topping in 90 regions (as of 2026).
